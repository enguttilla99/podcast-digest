  • Decacorn: a private company valued at over $10 billion.
  • Center corn: a private company valued at over $100 billion.
  • K-shape: refers to the idea that companies are either highly successful or struggling, with few in between.
  • Trillion-dollar club: refers to companies with a market capitalization of over $1 trillion.
